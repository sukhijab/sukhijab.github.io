Hi!

I am currently pursuing a PhD in computer science at ETH Zürich. I am co-supervised by Prof. Andreas Krause and Prof. Stelian Coros. My research interests are Reinforcement learning, Safe learning, and Robotics. Prior to my PhD I completed a BSc in Mechanical Engineering at ETH and a masters in Robotics at ETH as well.

Besides research, I enjoy playing football and support Liverpool FC.
